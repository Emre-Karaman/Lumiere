Breakfast Recipies

Menemen: Yesim Emecen

Ingredients for 3:

  • egg x2
  • green pepper x2
  • tomato x4
  • olive oil 4 Tbsp
  • salt to preference
  • black pepper to preference
  • paprika to preference
  • Bread to your preference

    • Instructions:

  • dice the tomatoes
  • cut the green pepper
  • crack and scramble eggs in a bowl
  • In a large pan cook the green peppers with olive oil on medium heat for 5 minutes
  • Add the diced tomatoes to the pan and cook for around 10 minutes until the tomatoes water evaporates
  • add all the spices and the eggs and cook until eggs are cooked to your preference
  • Serve with toasted bread

    • Black Sea Pide: Aysegul Karaman

    Ingredients for one large Pide for 1-2 people.

  • Minced Meat (Preferably 15 - 20% fat)
  • onions
  • water
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • chili (not necessary)
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • olive oil (might be necessary)

    • instructions

  • Sift the flower than add the yeast and hot water
  • mix till combined then add salt
  • Gently mix with your fingers to form a dough, it will be sticky at that stage.
  • Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and then knead it for 4-5 minutes or until it doesn't stick to your hands anymore.
  • in a bowl cover the dough with cling film and let it rest till it rises to double its current size
  • In a large pan cook the ground beef on medium heat till brown
  • Depending on the fat ration of your minced meat you can add the diced onions straight to the pan or add olive oil to pan first then add the onions and cook till translucent
  • Add salt, pepper, and chili flake to your meat mix then take it off of the heat
  • Now that your dough is rested and meat is ready split the dough to 4 equal pieces
  • roll out each piece to an oval shape making sure its not too thin so that it can hold the meat without breaking
  • add 1/4th of your meat filling to each piece of dough leaving around 1-2cm of space on the edge
  • Then carefully close the filling by folding both sides into each other making sure to pinch the dough at the top leaving no holes
  • Preheat the oven to 220 ℃ - 430 ℉
  • Once the oven is heated up put in your pide on a tray with baking paper and cook for around 15 minutes or until the outside is golden brown
  • Once you have taken out the pide from the oven and brush the entire thing with butter
