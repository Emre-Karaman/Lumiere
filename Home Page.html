List of Contributers:

Aysegul Karaman

Mother of two sons with a very big passion for cooking and especially cooking healthy alternatives of traditional Turkish cuisines. She is confident in her cooking ability and had many homemade recipies for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and Dessert Some of her recipies are passed down from her mother and have been perfected throughout many different generations, wheras some of are made by herself and inspired through different videos, websites, and even restaurants through dishes she has seen online or tasted.

Mother of two kids a daughter and son. Her passion for food is unmatched always coming up with new recipies and constantly learning through trial and errror and online videos and shows. Gulseren Celikdis

A well known fact is that grandparents have the best recipies and it is no exception for this woman. Her Turkish recipies are on another level and she has dishes from many different parts of Turkey. Gulcin Karaman

A grandmother who has spent her entire life in loving with cooking for her family and friends her pastries are incredibly well known in her town. Perfecting her touch over the years shes exhillerated to share her pastry recipies with the poeple of the future generation.
